# Introduction  
Welcome to my project. Below is a semi-comprehensive list of large changes imposed by this modpack. For more details on any specific feature, reference the mods in question.  
##   
## 1. Diet & Metabolism  
Focuses on long-term health and the physical effects of eating.  
* **Nutritional Benefits:** Players must balance food groups to gain health and speed buffs (**Diet**).  
* **Nutritional Transparency:** Provides detailed HUD overlays for saturation and hunger values (**AppleSkin**).  
* **Metabolic Effects:** Certain foods provide "Comfort" or "Nourishment" buffs that stall hunger depletion (**Farmer's Delight**, **Hunger's Delight**).  
  
## 2. Crop Agriculture & Gardening  
Expands how plants grow and how players interact with the land.  
* **Automated Maintenance:** Villagers and players can harvest and replant crops in one motion (**Smarter Farmers**, **Simply Harvesting**).  
* **Exotic Flora:** Adds specialized crops like rice, tomatoes, and tea, plus Nether/End-specific vegetation (**Farmer's Delight**, **End's Delight**, **Nether's Delight**, **Cultural Delights**).  
* **Visual Growth:** Crops have more distinct growth stages and interact with the environment (**Breezy**, **Supplementaries**).  
  
## 3. Culinary Arts & Gastronomy  
Moves cooking from a crafting grid to physical workstations.  
* **Station-Based Prep:** Requires cutting boards, pots, and skillets to create complex meals (**Farmer's Delight**, **Corn/Crabber's/Ocean's/Pineapple/Pizza/Rustic/Silent's Delight**).  
* **Professional Baking:** A deep system for dough preparation, oven baking, and pastries (**[Let's Do] Bakery**).  
* **Outdoor Cooking:** Adds specialized barbeque mechanics for large-scale meat prep (**Barbeque's Delight**).  
  
## 4. Viticulture & Brewing  
A dedicated system for the production of beverages and fermentation.  
* **Winemaking:** Includes grape growing, stomping, and aging wine in barrels (**[Let's Do] Vinery**).  
* **Fermentation:** Adds the ability to brew ales, stews, and specialized teas with various effects (**Brewin' And Chewin'**, **Farmer's Respite**).  
  
## 5. Livestock & Butchery  
Changes how animals are handled and processed for resources.  
* **Physical Carcasses:** Animals no longer drop "items" instantly; you must carry and process the carcass ( **Butchery LITE**).  
* **Fauna Diversity:** Wild and domestic animals have hundreds of visual variations and more complex behaviors (**Naturalist**, **More Mob Variants**).  
  
## 6. Combat & Weaponry  
Replaces "click-and-hit" with an animation-driven, rhythmic combat style.  
* **Dynamic Movesets:** Adds unique attack animations, dual-wielding, and "streak" combos (**Better Combat**).  
* **Expanded Arsenal:** Introduces specific classes of weapons like Katanas, Claymores, Muskets, and Daggers (**Simply Swords**, **Musket Mod**, **Basic Weapons**).  
* **Visual Feedback:** Adds pop-up damage numbers and screen-shake for impactful hits (**Damage Numbers**, **CameraOverhaul**).  
  
## 7. Enchanting & Forging  
Overhauls how gear is created, modified, and upgraded.  
* **Revamped Enchanting:** Replaces the RNG table with a manuscript-making process to collect enchanted books ( **Ancient Manuscripts**).  
* **Forging**: Tools and weapons must be forged in a semi-realistic way (**Hot Iron**)  
  
## 8. Movement & Parkour  
Changes how the player moves through the physical space of the world.  
* **Acrobatics:** Adds sliding, wall-running, vaulting, and ledge-grabbing (**ParCool!**, **ParCool+**, **Jump Over Fences**).  
* **Posture & Stance:** Players can physically sit, crawl, or sneak with realistic animations (**SittingPlus**, **Simply Walk**, **Simply Sneak**).  
  
## 9. Transportation & Logistics  
Introduces complex methods for moving goods and players across long distances.  
* **Road Networks:** Provides tools and incentives for building connected road systems (**Via Romana**).  
* **Caravans & Shipping:** Adds horse-drawn carts, wagons, and various-sized ships for bulk transport (**AstikorCarts Redux**, **Trotting Wagons**, **aleki's Nifty Ships**).  
* **Gliding:** Adds a physics-based glider for aerial traversal (**Gliders**).  
* **Postal System:** A functional mail system with mailboxes and letters for player-to-player delivery (**Mighty Mail**).  
  
## 10. Equestrianism  
Deepens the bond and utility of horses.  
* **Horse Expertise:** Adds "Horse Whistles" for summoning and new UI for tracking horse stats (**Horse Whistle: Reloaded**, **Horse Expert**, **Horseman**).  
* **Wagon Attachment:** Allows horses to pull functional storage and transport units (**AstikorCarts Redux**).  
  
## 11. Environment & Survival Realism  
Forces the player to react to the world’s natural cycles.  
* **Temperature Regulation:** Players must stay warm in winter and cool in summer or face damage (**Cold Sweat**).  
* **Seasonal Cycles:** The world transitions through four seasons, affecting crop growth and leaf colors (**Serene Seasons**).  
* **Tactile Weather:** Wind physically pushes particles, and rain/snow have enhanced visual and sound physics (**Immersive Winds**, **Breezy**, **Particle Rain**, **Pretty Rain**, **Sound Physics Remastered**).  
  
## 12. Mapping & Surveying  
Replaces the "Magic Minimap" with tactile exploration tools.  
* **Paper Atlas:** Provides a book-based map that must be manually filled and marked (**Antique Atlas**).  
  
## 13. Architecture & Geometry  
Allows for more complex building shapes and interior design.  
* **Diagonal Construction:** Players can now place fences, walls, and windows on diagonals (**Diagonal Fences/Walls/Windows**).  
* **Lived-In Detailing:** Hundreds of "clutter" items like pillows, kitchenware, and furniture (**Handcrafted**, **Another Furniture**, **Beautify**, **Tanuki Decor**, **Re:Deco**, **[Let's Do] Furniture**).  
  
## 14. Archaeology & Discovery  
Fleshes out the "Archaeology" system into a full gameplay loop.  
* **Fossil Hunting:** Adds more sites, artifacts, and tools for excavating ancient history (**Better Archeology**).  
  
## 15. Commerce & Economy  
Changes how players acquire goods without crafting.  
* **Bounty Systems:** A board-based system for taking "quests" from villagers for rewards (**Bountiful**).  
* **Rare Traders:** Introduces specialized traders like Goblins for unique items (**Goblin Traders**).  
  
## 16. Storage & Organization  
Moves away from "chests in a line" toward specialized gear storage.  
* **Wearable Storage:** Adds backpacks and tool belts that appear on the player model (**Backpacked**, **Tool Belt**).  
* **Sacks**: Replaces shulker boxes with sacks, which are easier to craft but impose a penalty when carrying many (**Supplementaries, Supplementaries Squared**)  
  
## 17. Photography & Documentation  
Adds a way to capture the world visually.  
* **Film Photography:** Players can craft cameras, take photos, and develop them into physical items to hang on walls (**Exposure**).  
* **Field Research:** Documentation of wildlife and flora (**Field Guide**).  
  
## 18. Cosmetic & Social Systems  
Enhances how the player looks and interacts with others.  
* **3D Upgrades:** Layers skins for depth and adds capes with physics (**3d-Skin-Layers**, **WaveyCapes**).  
* **Communication:** Adds a ping wheel for pointing out objects and visual indicators for what other players are doing (**Ping Wheel**).  
* **Transmutation:** Allows players to change the appearance of armor without losing stats (**Transmog**, **Vanity Slots**).
